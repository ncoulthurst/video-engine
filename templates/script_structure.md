# SCRIPT STRUCTURE (V2)

- **Cold Open (0:00 - 0:45):** Start with intrigue. Introduce the subject. Then, build the "Sharp Hook."
- **ACT 1 - Origins:** Brief, punchy background. Use short sentences (10-16 words avg). 
- **ACT 2 - Rise:** Development years. Focus on "The Warning Signs" of greatness and chaos.
- **ACT 3 - Peak:** Tactical and statistical apex. Explain the "Heat Map" and specific player movement.
- **ACT 4 - The Historic Event:** Dedicated section for the defining match (e.g., 2017 Remontada, 2014 World Cup).
- **ACT 5 - Redemption & Legacy:** The final chapter and "The Brutal Truth" ending.
